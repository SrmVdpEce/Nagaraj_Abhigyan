FLEX UR FLEX Rules and Regulations:

Level 1: The Hunt Begins! 🔍🧩 Participants will be given a series of clues or riddles. Each clue will lead you to discover a specific electronic component. Quick thinking and smart deductions will help you identify the component within the stipulated time. Only those who solve the clues and find the component will move on to the next level!🕵️‍♂️

Level 2: The Real Challenge! 💡🔧 In Level 2, we will give some real-world problems. The participants should provide solutions for these real-world problems within the given time limit.⏳⌛

Team Size: 2-4 members per team 👥🤝 Time Duration: Each level will have a time limit to ensure a competitive and fair event
