Paper Presentation

Paper Presentation Rules and Regulations:

1. Each team will be given 15 minutes to present their topic.

2. Slides should not exceed 15 pages.

3. The slides must be relevant and closely related to the topic.

4. The presentation will be evaluated by a panel of judges based on the delivery, visuals, engagement, and clarity of the presentation

5. The team with the highest marks will be declared the winner.

Date: 24th September 2024
