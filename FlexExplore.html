FLEXPLORE

FLEXPLORE Rules and Regulations:

🔥 Ready to challenge your tech skills?💡 Join us for Flexplore👨🏻‍💻 Get set for Flexplore, a thrilling combination of digital logic design and an adventurous treasure hunt! 🔍 Solve puzzles, decode clues, and use your tech-savvy mind to locate components and build circuits. It's a perfect test of your problem-solving abilities, offering hands-on learning in a fun and competitive environment. Gear up for a tech-fueled adventure!

👥 Team Up: Form a team of 4 members . Teamwork and strategy will lead you to victory!🌟

🧩Gameplay: 🤔 Round 1 : Treasure Hunt with a Tech Twist! Solve clues, follow hints, and gather the necessary components and materials. Your speed and precision are key! 3. All robots must be powered by batteries with a maximum voltage of 24 volts.

🤓Round 2 : The Circuit Challenge! Using the hints provided, build a working circuit. The first team to complete it accurately wins!

⚔ Rules : 1. Maximum of 4 members per team. 2. Strict time limits for each round. 3. The best teams from Round 1 will advance to the final Circuit Challenge.
